Armand Zorn

Member of the German Parliament representing Frankfurt am Main I

Welcome!

My name is Armand Zorn. On 26 September 2021, I was elected to the German Parliament (Deutscher Bundestag) representing Frankfurt am Main I. I was born in Cameroon and moved to Germany in 2000.

My Motivation

Fighting for a just and sustainable future, I am committed to promoting equality, education, and social cohesion.

Background and Experience

I have acquired a strong educational and professional background, which provides me with the tools to serve the community effectively.

My Work

I am actively involved in parliamentary work, participating in committees and initiatives that focus on social justice, innovation, and international cooperation.

My Topics

My primary political topics include education, digitalization, equality, climate protection, and economic development in Germany and Europe.

Frankfurt

I am dedicated to local engagement in Frankfurt, supporting community projects, cultural initiatives, and civic participation to improve the quality of life in the city.
